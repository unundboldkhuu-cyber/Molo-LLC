Molo Mine – Khatanbulag sum, Dornogovi province

Molo Mine is in Khatanbulag sum in Dornogovi province, the heart of the Gobi Desert. It is 720 km from Ulaanbaatar and only 150 km away from China's land port Khangi-Mandal. 170 km of paved highway to Khangi-Mandal will be completed 2022 July. 300 km of railroad is planned to be completed in 2025.

  • Direct link to China marble and GCC markets.
  • Growing infrastructure corridor (highway + rail).
  • Efficient outbound logistics for blocks and GCC.
  • Favourable jurisdiction for mineral projects.
